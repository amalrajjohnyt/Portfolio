The Olam Markets app, a mobile order management tool designed specifically for Olam's customers in Africa, has revolutionized distributor ordering. This first-of-its-kind app simplifies the process with a user-friendly interface, allowing features like order creation, account review, and transaction history tracking - all accessible from their phones. Leveraging Magento as a headless commerce platform, the Olam Markets app delivers a seamless user experience while potentially utilizing Magento's robust backend for product management and customer data. Magento in the Cloud: Enhanced Performance & Scalability

This migration has resulted in a more secure, performant, and scalable Magento platform for our client.
